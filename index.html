    How it works

    1. Say or type your task. Tap the microphone 🎙️ and speak, or type it into the box.
    2. Pick a date and time for when you'd like your friendly reminder.
    3. Tap “Add to my ledger.” Your task appears in the list below, kept safe on this device.
    4. Need a change? Tap the ✏️ pencil on any task to edit its name or time — or tap the 🎙️ mic, say the task's name aloud, and it will jump to editing.
    5. When the time comes, the task glows and a gentle chime plays. (Allow notifications for a pop-up too.)
    Everything stays private on your own device 💛
